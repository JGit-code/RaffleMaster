How Dream Raffles Work

Simple, transparent, and fair. Here's everything you need to know.

1

Choose Your Prize

Browse our selection of premium prizes. From the latest tech to luxury cars, we have something for everyone.

2

Buy Your Tickets

Select how many tickets you want to purchase. More tickets mean a higher chance of winning!

3

Get Confirmation

Receive your raffle tickets by email with your unique ticket numbers for the draw.

4

Wait for the Draw

Each raffle has a closing date. The draw happens either when all tickets are sold or when the timer ends.

5

Winner Selection

We use a random number generator to select the winning ticket. The draw is recorded for transparency.

6

Collect Your Prize

If you win, we'll contact you within 24 hours to arrange delivery of your prize.
